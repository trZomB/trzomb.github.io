Ben Wilson

Ben is a Senior Editor for Windows Central, the home for everything related to Windows and the broader Microsoft ecosystem, including Xbox.

He's been involved with Windows for over 20 years, starting with a Windows 3.1 PC in his family home. These days, he builds and tests his own custom desktop PCs, and travels with a wide variety of Windows laptops. You'll find him on the front page of Windows Central, and across social media.
